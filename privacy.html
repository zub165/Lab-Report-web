1. Who this applies to

This policy covers the Saeed Lab mobile app (iOS and Android) and related lab-management web tools used by authorized laboratory staff to manage patients, tests, appointments, reports, and payments. The app is a business tool for licensed labs—not a consumer wellness or social app.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use your data for cross-app advertising or tracking.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Account and patient data

  • Staff: username, email, role, lab group / branch assignment
  • Patients: name, phone, date of birth, optional address, medical history, appointments
  • Lab operations: test orders, results, reports, images, payment and insurance records

3.2 Technical data

  • Device type, OS version, app version (support and security)
  • Network connection to our API (HTTPS)
  • Error logs needed to fix bugs (no ad tracking)

Location: We do not continuously track GPS. Location is not required unless you add such a feature later in your lab workflow.

4. How we use information

  • Authenticate staff and enforce role-based access
  • Store and sync lab and patient records (local device + cloud API)
  • Generate reports, invoices, and appointment schedules
  • Process payments (cash, insurance, or card via Stripe when configured per lab)
  • Optional Saeed Lab Pro subscription billed by Apple or Google (we do not receive your full card number from the stores)

5. Where data is stored

  • On device: Encrypted local database for offline use (per lab group scope)
  • On server: SaeedLab Django API at api.mywaitime.com (or your lab’s configured backend)
  • In transit: HTTPS/TLS

6. Third parties

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

  • Lab backend (api.mywaitime.com): Primary storage and sync for your laboratory
  • Stripe: When your lab enables card payments, Stripe processes payment data under their privacy policy; each lab branch may use its own Stripe account
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Platform subscription purchases and renewals
  • Legal: If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process

7. Your choices and rights

  • Access and correct data through the app (where your role permits)
  • Export data (CSV/JSON) where the app provides export tools
  • Delete account: in-app Settings → Delete account or email zub165@yahoo.com with subject “Account Deletion Request - SAEED Lab App”
  • Cancel Saeed Lab Pro in your Apple or Google subscription settings

Deletion timing: We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days. Medical and financial records may be retained longer where your jurisdiction requires labs to keep records (e.g. tax or healthcare record rules).

8. Retention

  • Active accounts: while the lab uses the service
  • Inactive staff accounts: per your lab administrator policy
  • Patient/lab records: as required by your laboratory’s legal and professional obligations

9. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. Patient records for minors may be entered by healthcare staff as part of lawful lab services.

10. International transfers

Data may be processed on servers in countries other than yours. We use contractual and technical safeguards appropriate to healthcare and business data.

11. Regulatory notice

Laboratories are responsible for their own compliance (e.g. HIPAA, GDPR, local health-privacy laws). We design the app to support secure lab operations but do not replace your legal or professional compliance program. We are not claiming third-party certifications (e.g. SOC 2) unless separately agreed in writing with your organization.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. The current version is always posted at this URL. Material changes may be noted in the app or by email to registered administrators.
